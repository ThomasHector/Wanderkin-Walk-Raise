Frequently asked questions

Do I have to walk to play?

Walking is the heart of the game — your steps power everything. But you can also collect a baseline of in-game energy through the daily check-in, so you can play even on a rest day.

Why does Wanderkin ask for Motion & Fitness access?

It uses your step count to turn real-world movement into in-game energy. That data is processed only on your device and never leaves it. See our Privacy Policy.

Does it drain my battery or use GPS?

No. Wanderkin reads your step count rather than tracking your location, so it's light on battery.

Is there a cost, ads, or in-app purchases?

No. Wanderkin has no ads, no in-app purchases, and no premium currency.

Where is my progress saved?

On your device. Deleting the app removes your save.
