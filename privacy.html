Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2026

Wanderkin is designed to run entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

Motion & step data

With your permission, Wanderkin reads your step count and motion activity only to power gameplay — your real-world steps become in-game energy. This data is processed on your device and is never uploaded, sold, or shared.

Game data

Your progress is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app deletes this data.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, reminders are scheduled locally on your device to encourage you to walk. No data is sent to us.

What we don't do

  • No user accounts and no login.
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or third-party trackers.
  • No advertising.
  • No data leaves your device.

Children

The app collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, we will update this page and revise the date above.
